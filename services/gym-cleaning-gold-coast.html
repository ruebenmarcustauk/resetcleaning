Why Reset Cleaning

"Gym cleaning is not residential cleaning with a mop. Rubber floors, upholstered equipment, high-touch surfaces, and the biological load of a training environment require specific products, protocols, and consistency. We built this service around what fitness facilities actually need."

Members who pick up skin infections, breathe irritating chemical residue, or train around a persistent odour problem do not complain — they leave, and they tell people why. The cleaning standard of a facility is a direct signal of how much its owner cares about the member experience.

Most commercial cleaning products are not safe for the surfaces members grip, sit on, and lie on with bare skin. We use plant-derived antimicrobials that clean effectively without leaving synthetic residue on equipment, mats, or rubber flooring.

I

Equipment-protocol trained

We do not spray every surface with the same product. Rubber flooring, upholstered equipment, free weights, mirrors, and high-touch reception surfaces each require different products and technique. Our teams are trained on gym-specific protocols.

II

Timetable-aware scheduling

Your 5:30am class starts on time. We work backward from your first booking, not from our convenience — early morning before first class, post-close, or between sessions. We fit around your operation.

III

Odour management, not masking

Fragrance sprays mask odour for twenty minutes, then it returns worse. We use enzymatic and antimicrobial natural products that address the source — bacteria, sweat residue, and organic matter on equipment and flooring.
