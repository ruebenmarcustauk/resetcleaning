The turnover process

What happens between
check-out and check-in.

Every turnover follows the same sequence. Nothing is left to interpretation or memory. Your guests arrive to a property reset with the same precision every time — regardless of who was there before.

01

Inspection & linen strip

We note any damage, remove all linen, and assess the condition left by departing guests. Any issues are photographed and reported to you immediately.

02

Full clean & sanitise

Every surface, every bathroom, every kitchen element cleaned and sanitised with plant-derived products. High-touch areas — remotes, handles, switches — given specific attention.

03

Bed dress & amenity restock

Fresh linen dressed to hotel specification. Towels folded and displayed. Amenities restocked and arranged. The property is presented, not merely cleaned.

04

Scent finish & sign-off

Your signature scent is applied throughout the property as the final act. We photograph key areas and send you a completion confirmation before your guests arrive.
