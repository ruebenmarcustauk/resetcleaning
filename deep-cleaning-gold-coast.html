When to book

Six situations that
call for a deep clean.

Not every home needs a deep clean on the same schedule. Some need one immediately. Others benefit from one seasonally. Below are the most common reasons our clients reach out.

I

Moving into a new home

Even a professionally cleaned property for sale rarely meets the standard you deserve. A deep clean before you move in means you start with genuine cleanliness, not inherited build-up.

II

Post-renovation

Construction dust settles into every surface, gap, and vent. A deep clean after renovation removes the fine particulates that standard cleaning misses and leaves behind.

III

Between tenants

Bond cleans address the inspection standard. A deep clean goes further — ensuring the property is genuinely reset for new occupants, not merely passable for the property manager.

IV

Pre-holiday or event

Before guests arrive, a pre-sale preparation, or a significant occasion — a deep clean ensures your home is presented at its absolute best, beyond what routine cleaning achieves.

V

Seasonal reset

Even homes on regular cleaning schedules benefit from a deep clean one to two times per year. It catches what routine visits accumulate over months and keeps the baseline genuinely high.

VI

First Reset Homes visit

Many clients begin with a deep clean before moving to a regular schedule. It ensures every subsequent weekly or fortnightly clean starts from the right foundation — not a compromised one.
